Viewer (top-left)
  • Left-click (or touch) and hold to rotate.
  • Use mouse wheel (or two-fingers scroll) to zoom in and out.
  • Double-click (or double-tap) to highlight a specific sphere.
  • Holding shift while double-clicking (or double-tapping) will allow you to highlight 2 or 3 spheres.
  • If 2 (3) sphere are highlighted, an arrow (plane) is drawn to connect them.
Buttons (bottom-left)
  • Navigate to different views with the buttons.
  • Within the "Slice" view, use the arrow keys to move the slice plane.
  • The "Help" button shows this screen.
